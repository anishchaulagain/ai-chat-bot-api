# Platform Overview

Nexbizio is an institutional marketplace for buying and selling businesses —
a secure, vetted deal room where verified buyers and business owners connect
for high-value acquisitions and M&A transactions. Buyers discover off-market
listings and run NDA-gated diligence; sellers list their business and control
who sees sensitive details.

## Who It's For

- **Buyers** — investors and acquirers looking for vetted businesses to buy.
- **Sellers** — business owners ready to transition or sell.
- One account can act as both a buyer and a seller and switch between roles.

## Key Features

- **Marketplace & map search** — Browse listings for free and filter by
  industry, revenue, EBITDA, headcount, and location, including a map view.
- **NDA-gated listings** — Each listing has a public teaser (industry, summary,
  asking-price range, approximate location) plus members-only details
  (exact financials, legal name, full address) that unlock after an accepted
  NDA request.
- **Fully-public listings** — Sellers can mark a listing's full details public,
  so anyone can view the financials and location with no NDA or subscription.
  (Seller identity always stays private until messaging begins.)
- **Direct messaging** — Once an NDA is accepted, buyers message the seller in
  a private thread. Sellers can enable auto-accept so buyers go straight to
  chat.
- **Saved deals** — Buyers can favourite listings to revisit later.
- **Identity verification (KYC)** — Accounts can complete identity
  verification; verified sellers are badged.
- **Subscriptions** — Buyers subscribe to unlock NDA-gated diligence. Browsing
  is free; sellers list and transact for free.

## How It Works

**For buyers**
1. Sign up and verify your email.
2. Browse the marketplace for free.
3. Subscribe when you're ready to start NDA-gated diligence.
4. Request access on a listing; once the seller accepts, the full details
   unlock and you can message the seller directly.

**For sellers**
1. Sign up and verify your email.
2. Create a listing — fill in the public teaser and the members-only details.
3. Publish it. Review and accept NDA requests (or turn on auto-accept).
4. Optionally make a listing's details fully public.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I sign up?**
A: Create an account with your email, then click the verification link we send
to activate it.

**Q: Is browsing free?**
A: Yes. Anyone can browse the marketplace for free. Buyers need a subscription
to unlock NDA-gated details and request access. Sellers list and transact for
free.

**Q: What does it cost?**
A: Pricing applies to buyer subscriptions only; sellers use the platform for
free. See the Pricing/Billing page in the app for current plans.

**Q: How do I see a listing's full financials and location?**
A: Subscribe as a buyer, request NDA access on the listing, and once the seller
accepts, the members-only details unlock. Some listings are fully public and
need no NDA.

**Q: How do I contact a seller?**
A: After your NDA is accepted, open the listing and choose "Message seller" to
start a private conversation. The seller's identity is only shared inside that
thread.

**Q: Can I be both a buyer and a seller?**
A: Yes. A single account can hold both roles and switch the active role from
the user menu.

**Q: I can't get past sign-in / didn't get my verification email.**
A: On the verification screen you can resend the email. Check spam, and make
sure you're using the same address you signed up with.
